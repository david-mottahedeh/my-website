About

1954
My father Emanuel Mottahedeh was born in Esfahan, Iran.
1962
My mother Simin Shalom Mottahedeh was born in Golpayegan, Iran.
1983
They married in Esfahan and lived together in Golpayegan, my sister was born there later.
1985
They moved to Tehran, my brother was born there later.
1989
My mother sister and brother escaped from Tehran to Istanbul to Jerusalem.
1989
My father escaped from Tehran to Karachi to Geneva to Jerusalem.
1994
Town of Modi'in was established, I was born in Jerusalem.
1997
We moved from Jerusalem to Modi'in.
2012–Present
I moved to Tel Aviv, I live and work there since.
